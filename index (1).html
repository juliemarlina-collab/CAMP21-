Who Is This For?

Camp21 brings together diverse changemakers who care about heritage and the future.

🎓

Students

Polytechnic and college students ready to learn, collaborate and innovate across 10 cross-institution teams.

👩‍🏫

Lecturers

Guide, mentor and support students through the Design Thinking journey as accompanying facilitators.

⚖️

Judges

Industry and academic experts who evaluate, provide feedback and inspire outstanding student solutions.

⚙️

Organisers

The backbone team from JPPKK and Politeknik METrO KL ensuring a smooth, impactful 4-day experience.

Programme Highlights

Four days of learning, creating and celebrating cultural impact.

🏛
Day 1 — Monday
Discover & Empathise
  • Heritage immersion at Kompleks Kraf
  • AI Talk — Ts. Sathya a/p Manoharan
  • Pitching Talk — Encik Prabagaran
  • Empathy interviews with artisans
  • KL Hop-On Hop-Off exploration
🔍
Day 2 — Tuesday
Define & Ideate
  • Problem definition workshop
  • Ideation sprints with AI tools
  • Motif exploration & concept sketching
  • Mind mapping & concept development
🔧
Day 3 — Wednesday
Prototype & Test
  • Prototyping workshop
  • Tie Dye craft workshop
  • User testing & iteration
  • Pitch & storytelling preparation
🏆
Day 4 — Thursday
Pitch & Gallery Walk
  • 10 groups present (15 min each)
  • Panel: Dr. Siti Zubaidah (Ketua Juri)
  • Panel feedback session
  • Closing ceremony & prize giving
