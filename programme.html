Venue & Getting Here

Kompleks Kraf Kuala Lumpur

Jalan Conlay, 50450 Kuala Lumpur. The Conlay MRT Station is directly opposite — easiest way to arrive.

📍 Venue

🗺 Map embed — Kompleks Kraf Kuala Lumpur
  • MRT: Conlay Station is directly opposite Kompleks Kraf — easiest route
  • Pick-up Point 1: Avenue K Drop-Off Bay, Jalan Ampang entrance
  • Pick-up Point 2: Pavilion KL Bus / Coach Bay, Jalan Bukit Bintang side
  • Pick-up service departs at 7:00 am sharp each day

🏨 Recommended Accommodation

  • Royale Chulan KL — 0.20 km · From RM235 · Tel: 03-2688 9688
  • Pullman KL City Centre — 0.24 km · From RM340
  • KLoe Hotel — 0.3 km · From RM260
  • J-Hotel by Dorsett — 0.3 km · From RM180
  • Accommodation expenses are not covered by organiser

🍽 Meals

  • Breakfast, lunch and dinner provided for participants only
  • Dietary requirements collected at registration
  • Lecturers and guests — self-arranged
